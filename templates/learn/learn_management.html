Security Management Practices

What is security management?

It entails the identification of an organization's information assessment and the development, documentation, and implementation of policies, standards, procedures, and guidelines that ensure confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

What are the 4 key characteristics to create a good policy?
  1. Affordability: Cost efficient and does not incur too much effort to implement
  2. Functionality: Available security mechanism to support the policy
  3. Cultural issues: Gels with people's expectations, working style and beliefs
    • E.g. Use of email, social medias at work
  4. Legality: Meets the legal requirements
    • E.g. Usage of 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) in Internet banking
How do we succesfully implement a security policy?
  • Explain the security to all concerned
    • Relevant stakeholders understand the need for the policy
  • Outline everybody's responsibilities
    • To let them know what and how they are protecting the information
  • Usage of simple language (Make it clear and easy to understand)
  • Establish accountability
  • Provide for exceptions and periodic view
    • Enable the policy to be continuously updated and improved
