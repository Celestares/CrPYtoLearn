Types of Attacks

What are the different types of attack?
  • Passive attacks
  • Active attacks
Passive attacks

Credit to Russell Carroll slides from slideplayer.com

Does not involve any modifications to the contents of an original message. (Affects confidentiality)

  • Release of message content: Attacker sends message to someone against sender's wish
  • Traffic analysis: Attempts to analyse encoded messages to make sense of them
Active attacks

Credit to Russell Carroll slides from slideplayer.com

Involves modification to the contents of the original message.

  • Interruption (DOS): Affects availability of information/system
  • Modification: Affects integrity of message
    • Replay attack: Capture and resend message to confuse the destination host
    • Alteration: Capture and modify the content of the message
  • Fabrication (Masquerade): Affects authenticity of the communication
